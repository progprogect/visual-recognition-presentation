Implementation Options

Two approaches to integrate the system into your production line

Vibrating Table System

Standard Solution

How It Works:

  1. Worker loads nuts from a bag onto the vibrating table
  2. Vibration automatically distributes nuts evenly across the surface
  3. Overhead camera captures and classifies each nut in real-time
  4. Results are displayed on the dashboard
  5. Nuts are collected back into bags (sorted or unsorted)
Throughput: Up to 1 ton/hour
Labor: Manual loading/unloading required
Setup: Uses existing vibrating table (minimal modifications)
Best For: Smaller batches, flexible scheduling

Conveyor Belt System

Recommended

How It Works:

  1. Nuts are automatically loaded from a container onto the vibrating conveyor belt
  2. Belt moves continuously, vibration ensures even distribution
  3. Camera system captures and classifies nuts as they pass
  4. Classified nuts proceed to automatic packaging systems
  5. Full automation - no manual intervention required
Throughput: Significantly higher (scalable)
Labor: Fully automated - no manual work
Integration: Connects to automatic packaging
Best For: High-volume production, continuous operation

Key Advantages:

  • ‚úì Significantly increased processing capacity
  • ‚úì Complete automation of the entire process
  • ‚úì Can handle large volumes from containers
  • ‚úì Accelerates downstream packaging operations
